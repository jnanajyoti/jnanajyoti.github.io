I was born and raised in Delhi, having moved to the US in 2017 to do my PhD. I have a plethora of interests and hobbies outside of math, some of which I am absolutely passionate about. My love for sports is second to none, playing sports and working out invigorates me and sustains me. I was a professional badminton player back home and have represented Delhi at the national level many times. My favourite sport these days is football (soccer). I also love travelling, music, dance and stand up comedy, in no particular order of preference.

I am a sucker for novelty, so I really enjoy trying out new things, learning new skills and going on new adventures.
