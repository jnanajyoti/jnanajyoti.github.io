I was born and raised in Delhi, having moved to the US in 2017 to do my PhD. I have a plethora of interests and hobbies outside of math, some of which I am absolutely passionate about. I am a sucker for novelty, so I really enjoy trying out new things, learning new skills and going on new adventures.
